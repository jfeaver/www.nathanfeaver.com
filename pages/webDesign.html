My Philosophy about Personal Websites

I make websites for people that want to boost their online presence as an integral strategy in their job search. This means that I cater to a hiring manager audience and focus on creating simple pages that get to the point quickly and highlight your unique skills. I will meet with you to learn about who you are, what you want to achieve, and build a website to give you the upper edge in today's job market.

The websites that I build for people are typically 2-3 pages that show employers a fuller extent of your expertise and value than a simple resume, showing skills, character, portfolio, and more. I build websites in WordPress and provide WordPress training for my customers so that they can continue to easily update their website without purchasing more development time.
